Highest-Impact Money Levers

1. Capture serious buyers before they leave

Add AI chat, quote forms, phone click tracking, financing clicks, and appointment booking. A buyer looking at a $59,997 product should never be left with only Add to cart.

2. Sell the business case

Clinics need to understand how the bed can become a premium paid service. Build an ROI calculator, session pricing guide, and one-room revenue model.

3. Turn ads into specific landing pages

Do not send every ad to the homepage. Send med spa ads to a med spa page, financing ads to financing, and competitor-search ads to comparison pages.

4. Follow up automatically

High-ticket buyers rarely buy on the first visit. Email/SMS should answer objections, show proof, explain financing, and push to a specialist call.

5. Retarget by intent

Product-page visitors, financing-page visitors, comparison-page visitors, and quote-form abandoners should all see different follow-up ads.

6. Measure sales, not just clicks

The owner needs one report showing ad spend, clicks, quote requests, calls, financing applications, booked calls, deals, and closed revenue.

AI Sales Assistant Prototype

This is a static demo for review. The production version should connect to a real AI backend, approved product knowledge, quote forms, financing links, appointment booking, and CRM lead creation.

What the AI assistant should do

  • Ask whether the visitor is a med spa, clinic, chiropractor, gym, recovery room, salon, or home buyer.
  • Qualify budget, timeline, room type, location, financing interest, and number of expected weekly sessions.
  • Answer safe product questions using approved specs: 10/15/20-minute sessions, red/NIR wavelengths, LED count, dimensions, warranty, training, shipping, support.
  • Route high-intent buyers to Request Quote, Apply for Financing, Talk to Specialist, or Book Demo.
  • Create CRM leads with source, page, answers, urgency, product interest, and next best action.
  • Escalate medical, legal, or treatment-result questions to a human with compliant language.

Follow-Up System

Quote request sequence

  • Instant confirmation.
  • Rep alert.
  • Spec sheet email.
  • Financing option email.
  • Case/use-case email.
  • Book-call reminder.

Financing visitor sequence

  • Payment-path explanation.
  • Business-case calculator.
  • What to prepare before applying.
  • Specialist-call CTA.

Comparison visitor sequence

  • Buyer checklist.
  • Panel vs bed explanation.
  • Questions to ask vendors.
  • OvationULT spec proof.

Abandoned form sequence

  • "Still have questions?" email.
  • Short SMS prompt if consented.
  • Direct calendar link.
  • Human rep follow-up.

Cold lead nurture

  • Education every 5-7 days.
  • Facility use cases.
  • Revenue room ideas.
  • Invite to consultation.

Sales rep tasks

  • Call hot leads within 5 minutes.
  • Text after missed calls.
  • Send quote summary.
  • Log outcome and objection.

90-Day Execution Plan

Week 1: Fix sales path

Add quote, financing, and specialist-call CTAs to homepage and product pages. Fix broken resource links. Add tracking plan.

Week 2: Launch AI lead capture prototype

Install production chatbot or lead-assistant widget. Add approved knowledge base and CRM notifications. Start with text chat before voice.

Weeks 3-4: Build landing pages

Launch commercial bed, med spa, financing, comparison, and buyer-checklist pages. Connect every page to quote and calendar paths.

Month 2: Launch ads and retargeting

Run the 15 ad concepts, Google Search campaigns, Meta retargeting, LinkedIn owner ads, and YouTube demo cuts.

Month 2: Add follow-up automation

Create quote, financing, comparison, abandoned form, and cold lead sequences. Alert reps on high-intent leads.

Month 3: Optimize by pipeline

Shift budget based on booked calls and qualified opportunities. Add new creative around the top objections and highest-intent audiences.
