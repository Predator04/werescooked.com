Winning Angles To Recreate

Revenue room

Speak to owners: one room, premium sessions, fast cleanup, quote/financing path.

10-minute treatment

Compete against slower or clunkier sessions by making throughput visible.

Zero-gravity comfort

Show the bed as a premium client experience, not a commodity red panel.

Spec proof

Use 28,443 LEDs, 635nm red, 850nm NIR, and 65 mW/cm2 as credibility anchors.

Built for operators

Standard outlet, 30-second wipe-down, ships assembled, training included.

Better than gadgets

Against mask, wrap, bulb, and panel curiosity traffic, show why serious buyers need full-body commercial coverage.

How We Get More Clicks Than Generic Red Light Ads

Higher-intent targeting

Most red light ads draw home-device shoppers. We target people searching for commercial beds, med spa equipment, red light bed financing, and clinic recovery revenue.

More specific hooks

"28,443 LEDs," "10-minute sessions," "30-second reset," and "zero-gravity red light bed" beat broad red-light curiosity hooks.

Better post-click path

The ads need to land on quote, financing, and specialist-call CTAs. Fixing the current broken/unclear paths is part of making the clicks convert.

Launch Checklist

  • Fix the homepage and product-page CTAs before spending. Paid clicks should not land on the current add-to-cart-only path for a high-ticket product.
  • Create one quote landing page and one comparison/checklist lead magnet page.
  • Install/verify Google Ads, GA4, Meta pixel, phone-click, form-submit, financing-click, and quote-click tracking.
  • Launch 6 Meta ads first, 3 Google Search ad groups, and 2 LinkedIn owner/operator ads. Hold the rest for week-two refresh.
  • Judge winners on qualified quote requests and booked calls, not only clicks.
