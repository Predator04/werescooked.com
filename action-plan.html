Action Plan

The cleanest path from review to more qualified sales conversations

A focused execution plan that turns the review into concrete work, measurable results, and owner decisions.

First 14 daysNext 45 daysScale after proof

1. Fix the obvious revenue leaks

  • Add quote, financing, and specialist CTAs.
  • Fix broken resource URLs.
  • Clean duplicate/stale pages.
  • Set one final contact/quote destination.

2. Build sales pages

  • Commercial red light bed page.
  • Med spa equipment page.
  • Recovery room/gym page.
  • Financing page.
  • Comparison page.
  • Buyer checklist page.

3. Add capture and follow-up

  • AI lead assistant.
  • Quote form.
  • Calendar/specialist call.
  • Email/SMS follow-up if consented.
  • Sales rep alerts.

4. Launch controlled campaigns

  • Google Search for high intent.
  • Meta retargeting and creative testing.
  • LinkedIn owner/operator ads.
  • YouTube walkthrough videos.

Final recommendation

Do not scale traffic until the funnel can catch and qualify buyers. Approve the sales-system build first, then launch ads with tracking and weekly reporting.

Action plan is designed for measured execution, not broad ad spend without attribution.
