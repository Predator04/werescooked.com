Launch Readiness

What can launch now, what needs approval, and what should wait

A clear readiness checklist for turning the review into action without creating compliance, tracking, or sales-follow-up risk.

Executive action checklistAd and sales funnel readinessUse before budget approval

Ready now

  • Executive review pages.
  • Competitor sales-pattern review.
  • 15 original ad concepts.
  • Revenue model calculator.
  • AI assistant plan and static prototype.
  • 90-day roadmap.

Needs approval

  • Final product specs.
  • Medical/FDA wording.
  • Warranty and training claims.
  • Financing details.
  • Ad budget and channels.
  • Who handles lead follow-up.

Do not scale yet

  • Large ad spend before tracking.
  • Medical claims without review.
  • Ads pointed to broken/weak pages.
  • Lead capture without fast callback.
  • Campaigns judged only by clicks.

Recommended Approval

Approve a two-phase rollout: first fix the sales path and tracking, then launch a controlled ad test into the new pages. This avoids wasting spend while still moving quickly.

Launch readiness is based on public-site review and available local evidence. Final readiness depends on account access and owner-approved claims.
